  • 1842 -

  • 1843 - 

    Son, Robert Todd Lincoln, is born

  • 1849 - 

    Admitted to the bar of the United States Supreme Court

  • 1850 - 

    Son, William Wallace Lincoln, is born

  • 1853 - 

    Son, Thomas Lincoln, is born

  • 1860 - 

    Nominated as the Republican party's candidate for president of the United States

  • 1861 - 

    Inaugurated as sixteenth president

  • 1863 - 

    Issued Final Emancipation Proclomation

  • 1865 - 

    Assassinated by gunshot
